Fragments of the Future

This map allows for us to invision a world of the Cyberpunk Aesthetic and imagine what it would be like in the future. I took inspiration from Sci-Fi genre as a whole, which is a genre I am closely tied to due to sentimental reason, but specifically movies, such as Gattaca (1997) and Blade Runner (1982). This map has allowed me to delve into my research skills, with datasets that I thought might be interesting and related, and get creative, with trying to figure out which colors to use. I found that this map also relly challenged me to make acessable, because many colors often seen in Sci-Fi movies are not color blind acessable.
