From Fabric to Fallout: Fast Fashion and the Environment

This StoryMap explores the pervasive issue of fast fashion's environmental and social impacts through an engaging visual narrative. I recently started getting into fashion recently and I wanted to delve into the unsustainable practices that drive fast fashion, from the overuse of water and chemicals in textile production to the massive amounts of waste generated by discarded clothing. I wanted to understand what the potential of where my money is going to, such as manufacturing and waste disposal, underscoring the stark contrast between the economic benefits for producers and the environmental toll so many communities. Through interactive maps, infographics, and compelling storytelling, I wanted to call for greater awareness and action to adopt more sustainable practices in the fashion industry, urging consumers, corporations, and myself alike to rethink their relationship with clothing.
