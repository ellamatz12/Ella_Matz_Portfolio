Journey of Our Ancestors: Pathways to Early Human Migration

This map traces the extraordinary journey of early human migration, illustrating the pathways that our ancestors took over tens of thousands of years to populate the globe. It highlights significant archaeological sites and their estimated timelines, including the origins in Africa approximately 200,000 years ago and subsequent dispersal into Europe, Asia, Australia, and the Americas. I have always been fascinated by archaeology, and while taking an anthropology class, I gained valuable insights that deepened my understanding of this map.
